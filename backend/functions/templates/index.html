A gift card for the Internet

Hello there!

This website serves as a demo of Gib. Eventually, you will be able to create a web monetized link for any site! However, currently we've created a mock page (the Demo tab) which represents a website with premium content that is hidden behind a paywall. By following the below steps, you will be able to generate and share Gib links that provide access to the premium content! Note: In our next iteration, you will be able to generate links using a browser extension.

Step 1

Click the Demo tab. You'll see a paywall blocking the premium content on the page.

Step 2

Click the Gib Link tab (for the Demo, we've hard coded the example URL).

Step 3

Enter the amount of time (in seconds) that you want to be able to access the premium content for.

Step 4

Click the “Generate Link” button.

Step 5

Copy and paste the link into your browser's URL bar, or send it to someone.

Step 6

You or the person you've shared the link with can click the link and see the premium content behind the paywall!
